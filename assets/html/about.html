This application is intended to prove concept of optical character recognition. It is comprised of 3 parts: pre-processing, recognition and post-processing.
In this app, pre-processing part is done by the process of image optimization. It is to prepare the image for recognition process at optimum level. We do a round of grayscale effect on the image before it is send to recognition phase.
In recognition phase, we send the bitmap of the image to the Tesseract engine to perform optical character recognition. The result of the recognition is send to the user.
In post-processing phase, the result of the optical character recognition is filtered from invalid symbols in a word.
